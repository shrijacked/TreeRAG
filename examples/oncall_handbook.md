# On-Call Handbook

## Shift Handoffs

At the start of each shift, review the unresolved alerts, confirm the current incident status, and acknowledge any pending pages in PagerDuty before taking ownership.

## Incident Command

For active Sev-1 incidents, the incident commander coordinates responders, assigns note-taking, and keeps the Jira incident timeline accurate. Escalation policy still follows the dedicated incident runbook.

## Access Escalations

If the on-call engineer needs temporary production access, request it through the access workflow and include the incident ticket in the approval request.

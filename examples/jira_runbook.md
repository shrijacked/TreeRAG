# Jira Incident Runbook

## Severity Levels

Sev-1 incidents involve broad customer impact or critical service outage. The primary incident commander must open a Jira incident ticket immediately and update the status page within five minutes.

## Escalation Policy

For Sev-1 incidents, page the primary on-call engineer immediately. If there is no acknowledgment within five minutes, escalate to the secondary on-call and the engineering manager. Record every escalation step in the Jira incident timeline.

## Notification Rules

For Sev-1 incidents, notify support leadership and customer success once engineering acknowledges the incident. Add external communication notes to the Jira ticket before sharing updates broadly.

# Access Management Runbook

## Approval Rules

Standard production access requires manager approval, a ticket reference, and a documented reason for the requested scope.

## Emergency Access

Emergency production access can be granted immediately for active Sev-1 incidents, but the approval record must be backfilled within 24 hours of the access grant.

## Access Revocation

Revoke temporary production access as soon as the incident is resolved and record the cleanup in the incident ticket before closing it.
